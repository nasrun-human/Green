Product functions and advantages

The scan button is built in the profile interface of opera system to realize one click import

Click the scan button in the built-in profile interface to import names, gender, birthday, and more through official interfaces.

Adopt the official interface of opera OPP_IDS to ensure the information security

Reads info through multi-functional scanning equipment and transmits via OPP_IDS interface, improving accuracy.

Store the photos of guest's certificates in opera and check them accurately

Stores ID photos in guest profile as attachments, facilitating identity checks with room cards.

Can filter the imported guest information

Supports user-defined filtering to avoid overwriting original opera system information.

Support team guests to register quickly to achieve accurate matching

Fast registration of team guests, reading info in batches, and matching IDs to room numbers accurately.

Support scanning ID card, passport and other related documents

Hardware supports second generation card readers and OCR card readers for various document types.
