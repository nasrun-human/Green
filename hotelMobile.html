Oracle Hospitality Hotel Mobile

PRODUCT

Enabling Mobile Hotel Operations

Oracle Hospitality Hotel Mobile is a native app that extends key Oracle Hospitality hotel functions to mobile devices, empowering your staff to improve guest engagement by offering personalized services virtually anywhere across your property.

Product Features

The Dashboard

The Dashboard provides summary information of the hotel activities in real time with drill down functionality.

Check-In and check-out

Differentiate the guest experience by effectively managing the check-in & check-out process using mobile PMS.

Mobile front desk operations

Increase your operational efficiency by untethering your front desk operation.

Streamline Housekeeping

Manage room status and track housekeeping activities.

Increase Maintenance Efficiency

Document and track maintenance tasks in real-time and upload a photo with a work order.

Enhanced Experience, Streamlined Operations

Enable mobile check-in and check-out

Enable mobile check-in and check-out: reservations search with intuitive filters, guest check-in, manage guest preferences, accompanying guests and sharers, room assignment, key encoding, posting charges and articles, check out with ability to email folio.

Streamline housekeeping

Get real-time updates when guests check out, prioritize rooms on queue, awareness of linen and towel change, manage room status, create maintenance tasks, enhance collaboration with the front desk.

Proactive maintenance

Manage and create maintenance tasks, include picture when creating maintenance task, update and resolve maintenance tasks.
