Product Features

The E-Signature system helps hotel customers to realize a paperless business during the process of check-in and check-out, which achieves an electronic management on documents generating and displays more information. All related documents can be easily stored on PC and hotel server directly.

Product functions and advantages

Save money

The electronic signature realizes the paperless operation, the customer only needs to sign on the electronic digital board, the signed document can be printed, saved, preview, download and so on, saves the paper cost, reduces the occupation of resources.

Easy to find and clean

The completed signed historical documents can be inquired by reservation confirmation number, name, date and other conditions. And only on the server to keep the number of days set by the hotel, due to automatically clear.
